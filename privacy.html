This Privacy Policy explains what information Pinevale App may collect, how that information is used, and how it is handled.

Pinevale App is a Discord application created to support Discord server communities with shoutout submissions, staff review workflows, community recognition posts, and related server tools.

Pinevale App is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Discord Inc. Discord may process user information according to its own Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collected

When users interact with Pinevale App, the app may collect and store limited information, including:

  • Discord user ID
  • Discord username or display name
  • Submitted message content
  • Optional identification provided by the user
  • Submission status, such as pending, approved, denied, or needs revision
  • Staff review decisions
  • Staff-provided reasons for denial or revision
  • Timestamps related to submissions and reviews
  • Basic warning flags generated by the app to help staff review submissions

Pinevale App does not intentionally collect passwords, payment information, private messages outside app interactions, or sensitive personal information.

2. How Information Is Used

Information collected by Pinevale App may be used to:

  • Process user submissions
  • Display submissions to authorized server staff for review
  • Post approved submissions in Discord channels
  • Prevent spam, abuse, or misuse
  • Enforce server rules and app rules
  • Maintain basic records of app activity
  • Improve app functionality and reliability

3. Public Information

If a submission is approved, the submitted message may be posted publicly in a Discord channel.

If the user provides optional identification, that identification may also be shown publicly with the approved submission.

If the identification field is left empty, Pinevale App will not publicly show a submitter name or identification line.

4. Staff Access

Authorized server staff may be able to view submission details, including the Discord user who submitted the message, the submitted content, optional identification, generated warnings, submission status, timestamps, and review history.

This access is used for moderation, approval, abuse prevention, and community management purposes.

5. Data Storage

Pinevale App may store data using local files, hosting storage, databases, or other infrastructure depending on how the app is deployed.

Data may be retained until it is deleted manually, removed during maintenance, lost during redeployment, or no longer needed for app operation or moderation purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data.

Information collected by Pinevale App is not shared with advertisers. Information may be visible to authorized server staff and may be processed by Discord as part of normal Discord platform operation.

7. Data Removal

Users may request removal of stored submission data by contacting the Pinevale App operator or the staff team of the Discord server where the app is used.

Some information may be retained when necessary for moderation, abuse prevention, security, or server safety.

8. Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect stored app data, but no system is completely secure. Users should not submit private, sensitive, or personal information through Pinevale App.

9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Pinevale App after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or Pinevale App data, contact the Pinevale App operator or the staff team of the Discord server where the app is used.

Pinevale App Privacy Policy, last updated 6/10/26.
